Joining the Gmvault-User mailing list

To be informed about new Gmvault releases, get some help on how to use Gmvault, get advanced tips and learn about common practices, etc, you can consult or join the Gmvault User Group.
Click here to consult previous messages or join the Gmvault-User Group with your Google account. You can also join Gmvault-User without a Google account just be sending an email to the Gmvault-User Group with in the subject, the word subscribe.
