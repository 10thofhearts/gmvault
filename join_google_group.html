Joining the Gmvault-Users mailing list


To join the Gmvault-Users group with your Google account, click here.
You can also join Gmvault-User without a Google account by sending an email to the Gmvault-Users group with in the subject, the word subscribe.
For more information refer to the Google Group Help page.
You can post here any questions/suggestions or problems reports you would have regarding Gmvault. We would of course also appreciate any love messages.
