Why donating for Gmvault ?


  • You like Gmvault and think that it is a very useful software and would like it to reach a very high quality level,
  • You would like new features such as a graphical interface, facilities for helping Google Business App admins managing 100 Gmail accounts or automatic cloud savings in the future versions,
  • You want to show your interest in having Gmvault maintained and supported professionally,
  • You want to support an open source project useful for millions of users,
  • You have your own motives and want to sponsor Gmvault

For all these reasons, please donate to Gmvault via Paypal or Flattr to Gmvault

Because of Paypal and Flattr fees, please consider giving 5$ (4 euros) or more to Gmvault. If you cannot donate the equivalent of 2 beers, we will graciously accept less.

We will graciously accept any donations, but because of the Paypal and Flattr fees, may we suggest you to give at least 4$ (3.5 euros or 1 and a half beer).
